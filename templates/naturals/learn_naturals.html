Learning the Basics: Naturals

Basic Information:

  • Naturals are played on the white keys of the piano!
  • The notes are: C, D, E, F, G, A, B, C
  • We say that between each natural note, it is a whole step! However, between E and F is a half note and between B and C is also a half note
    • This can be remembered because when looking at a keyboard, if there is a black key in between, it makes it a whole step, otherwise it's a half step
